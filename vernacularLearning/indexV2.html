About

To not be able to speak and understand English has become a determining factor for the opportunities available to a person to succeed in so many fields in India. For all the buzz around English Advantage in the country, the fact remains that less than 10% of the Indians are well-versed in the language. We can't expect to reach our full potential while more than 90% people are left behind as a design. We at Vernacular Learning work towards changing this trend by providing Technical Education and Training in the regional languages. While we lobby in favor of opportunities in more Indian languages at industry and government levels, we do provide English language training separately considering the current situation in the subject.

As a side note, we are not against study of any language. Studies show that bilingual/multilingual people show better cognitive skills. Moreover, sufficient proficieny in a language opens doors for new and different ideas from different regions of the world. Problem is with looking down on people for not knowing a language foreign to them and denying them opportunites in that name.
